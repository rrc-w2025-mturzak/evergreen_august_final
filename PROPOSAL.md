### Concept
I want to make an impound lot API. Basically I want to create and store the vehicle data that have been towed.
### Scope and Functionality: 
All basic endponts will relate to the impound lot.
I will be able to make data with POST that stores the license plate, vehicle type, color, the release fee, and how long its been in the lot
I will be able to GET both an individual vehicle and a list of all current vehicles
I will be able to DELETE a vehicle that has been picked up and is no longer in the lot
I will be able to alter the information of the vehicles, such as if the fee has changed with PUT
I will also have a health endpoint with GET
